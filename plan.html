Museum Shop

In addition to catalogs and books, jewelry and ceramics, the museum store features surprising design items. The Museum Shop is open when the museum is open, Tuesday through Sunday from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. You do not need a museum ticket to visit the shop.

Brasserie

Visit the brasserie for a cup of coffee or for lunch. It is the place to meet friends, family or business partners. Contact MuseumBrasserie for reservations or meetings for groups. Opening hours are: Tuesday through Sunday from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. You do not need a museum ticket.
